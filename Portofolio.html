Shoot with Film
While Digital Photography ensured fast and reliable photo taking, sometimes the purpose of photography itself being left out during the process, which is Capturing Moment.
People tend to Rush photo taking as an outcome of knowing that Digital Camera has little to none limitation on exposure as opposed on film, which is limited to 24 to 36 exposure per film roll. Therefore, shooting with film may or may not have caused the photographer to take and ensure the exposures were not in vain, and also the time taken on composing each shot is somehow more valued when the result is contenting.
